#### 复现结果（达到/超越论文）
- **Amazon**: F1-Macro 92.56%, AUC 98.16% (论文: 91.94%, 93.95%) ✅ 超越
- **YelpChi**: F1-Macro 76.16%, AUC 89.41% (论文: 75.68%, 89.67%) ✅ 达标
- **T-Finance**: F1-Macro 88.75%, AUC 95.96% (论文: 88.99%, 95.99%) ✅ 一致

#### ResAFA 创新结果
- **Amazon (同构 ResAFA)**: Recall 85.15% (+3.36%), F1-Macro 93.14% (+0.58%), AUC 97.82%
- **YelpChi (异构 ResAFA)**: Recall 68.48%, F1-Macro 76.68%, AUC 90.43% (最优综合性能)
